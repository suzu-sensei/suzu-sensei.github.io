安心の空間

間違えても
大丈夫。
ここは安全な場所。

很多學生學日文最大的障礙，不是文法——而是「怕說錯」。

在這裡，錯誤是學習的一部分。SUZU老師會用最溫和的方式引導你，讓你在真正放鬆的狀態下，自然地說出日文。

SUZU老師自己也曾學習英語、中文、泰語——自學了很久，卻在當地完全說不出口、無法和當地人對話，那種挫折感非常真實。
正因如此，上課時間就是輸入的時間：在打好基礎的同時，學習日本人真正在說的發音和活生生的單字，不再只是背課本。 For many students, the biggest barrier to learning Japanese isn't grammar — it's the fear of making mistakes.

Here, mistakes are part of the learning journey. SUZU guides you in the most encouraging way, so you can speak Japanese naturally in a truly relaxed space.

SUZU has personally studied English, Mandarin, and Thai — self-studying for years yet struggling to communicate with locals in the field. That frustration was very real.
That's exactly why every lesson is immersion time: building solid foundations while learning the authentic pronunciation and everyday vocabulary that Japanese people actually use — not just textbook phrases.

02

フリートーク

ライティング
